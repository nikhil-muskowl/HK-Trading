About Us

H K Trading and Consulting is newly emerging and reputed Environment, Mining and Legal consultancy to provide one point services to our cliental. With the competent team and highly experience people in the field, HKTC is emerging as the leading solution provider for the industry in the field of Environment, Mining and Legal services.

HKTC has developed a strong base of In-house technologies built up with diverse experience and various exposure of its multi-disciplinary Experts, Scientists and Technocrats.

The team HKTC in total has completed and approved more than 200 Mining Plans for both Major and Minor Mineral in State of Rajasthan, Himachal Pradesh, Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh and others and obtained Environment Clearances form MOEF & CC, New Delhi and different State Level Committees.

The Team Members were also engaged in different foreign projects and were consultants of Cement Plants, Mineral Processing Industries and other Manufacturing Industries for multiple areas of expertise.
